# Template Sections

Use this file when rendering `report.md`.

This v2 template is closer to a real dossier product, but still intentionally shorter than the full Ventify-style 20-chapter output.

## 1. Executive Summary

- 5 to 8 bullets
- Only the most decision-relevant points

## 2. Identity & Background

- Basic identity
- Education or prior background if relevant
- Location or operating base if public

## 3. Career History

- Career arc
- Important roles and transitions

## 4. Current Company & Role

- Current role
- Company status
- Ownership or control notes when visible

## 5. Digital Footprint

- Public social, code, media, and web traces
- Notable absences if meaningful

## 6. Signal Narrative

- Useful synthesis on strengths, concerns, and external signal quality
- Must remain evidence-constrained

## 7. Relationship Notes

- Key people
- Family or ownership links only when public and relevant
- Strategic counterparties or linked entities

## 8. Behavior Patterns

- Repeat patterns visible from public actions or reporting
- Keep these as evidence-backed tendencies, not personality fiction

## 9. Timeline

- Major career or company events
- Only meaningful dates

## 10. Gaps & Contradictions

- Missing facts
- Inconsistencies

## 11. Comprehensive Assessment

- What is proven
- What is only inferred

## 12. Risk Ledger

- Short, specific entries
- Category, severity, evidence

## 13. Source Register

- Compact but clear
- Prefer a table in the final report

## 14. Outreach & Research Backlog

- Public contact channels only
- Best next contact path
- Open research questions
