# Quality Bar

Use this file before final delivery.

## Claim rules

- `fact`
  - Directly supported by a strong source.
- `inference`
  - Reasonable synthesis from multiple signals, but not directly stated as such.
- `unverified`
  - Plausible but still insufficiently supported.

## Confidence rules

- `high`
  - One strong direct source or two independent strong sources.
- `medium`
  - One medium-quality source or several weaker sources pointing the same way.
- `low`
  - Weak or indirect support only.

## Writing rules

- Do not let persuasive language impersonate evidence.
- Keep risk statements specific and source-backed.
- Preserve unknowns.
- If a relationship or motivation is uncertain, say so plainly.
- Never invent private contact data.

## What good looks like

- Sources are visible.
- Risks are categorized.
- Important claims are traceable.
- The report still reads cleanly.
