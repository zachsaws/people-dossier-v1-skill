# Source Playbook

Use this file when planning searches and deciding source tiers.

## Search order

1. Resolve the target.
2. Bind the company and legal entities.
3. Search Tier 1 sources.
4. Expand to Tier 2 detail sources.
5. Use Tier 3 only for weak-signal enrichment or corroboration.
6. Record contradictions and missing facts in backlog items.

## Tier 1

- Registry and shareholder databases
- Filings and official announcements
- Government websites
- Company websites and official contact pages

## Tier 2

- Mainstream media
- Industry media
- GitHub
- Patent databases
- Hiring pages and job boards
- Product listings and official distribution pages

## Tier 3

- Social media
- Video channels
- Community and forum posts
- Investor or commentary platforms

## Query patterns

- `"person name" + company`
- `"person name" + role`
- `"person name" + filing`
- `"company name" + shareholder`
- `"company name" + legal representative`
- `"company name" + financing`
- `"person name" + interview`
- `"person name" + patent`
- `"company name" + contact`
- `"company name" + lawsuit`

## Tiering rule

- Prefer one Tier 1 source over many Tier 3 sources.
- Treat Tier 3 sources as hints unless corroborated.
- Keep stale official sources marked as lower confidence when freshness matters.
